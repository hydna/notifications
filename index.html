Instant Messaging Notfication

This is the Instant Messaging Notification icon. It keeps track of how many unread messages that is in queue.

To increase the indicator:

$ curl --data "incr"

To decrease the indicator:

$ curl --data "decr"

To reset the indicator:

$ curl --data "reset"

To set the indicator to a specific value:

$ curl --data "set 15"
